WHAT WE DO?

Repair Squad is an online platform that provide repair services for all kinds of device and gadgets. We are a specialist in repairs that tackle all forms of gadget damage – from cracked screens to broken buttons. We repair any and every form of device – from your MP3, to your tablet, smartphone or desktop PC. For owners of Apple devices, we offer a superfast service that spans to include IPhone, IPad, IPod, and Apple Computers.

PLACE AN ORDER

Using Repair Squad is simple expecially for a first time users you just give us your name and phone number and we do the calling. But for subsequent order placing, you click on the “Place An Order” tab (register and login if you dont already have an account) and fill in the required details. All the details required will give us all we need to process your order from start to finish. Once we receive your order, your gadget/device would be picked up, repaired and delivered back to you

TRACK YOUR ORDER

Repair Squad provides an order tracking functionality. For every repair order you placed, you are provided with a unique order ID, that you can use to track the status of the order at any time.
