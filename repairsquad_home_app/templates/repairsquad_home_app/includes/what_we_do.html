WHAT WE DO?

Repair Squad is a platform that allows you to effortlessly place a repair order for your computers, mobile phones, drones, smart TV and other gadgets via the internet.

PLACE AN ORDER

When you place an order on the Repair Squad platform, a courier agent is sent to pick up your device, we get it fixed and deliver back to you.

TRACK YOUR ORDER

Repair Squad provides an order tracking functionality. For every repair order you placed, you are provided with a unique order id, that you can use to track the status of the order at any time.
